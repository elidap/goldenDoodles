About Chikas GoldenDoodles

Chikas litter was whelped between 7:30pm-10pm April 12, 2017. This is the first picture I took once I made sure they were all out. 9 Total :]

The puppies were taken to their first Vet appointment at Kempsville Veterinary Hospital on April 15, 2017. Dr. Uvaerkaller (Chika's regular Vet) cleared their check up and said all babies were born healthy. She also praised Chika for being such a great mom and said she looked strong and took the delivery well.

The puppies have had their first set of shots (6 weeks) and were de-wormed at 2, 4, 6, and will be dewormed again at 8 weeks.

We know 8 weeks is the normal "rehoming" age however, for the sake of the puppies socialization, rehoming will take place on the 9th week (June 14th).

Meet the Parents

Mom
Chika

Chika is a Golden Retriever, she was whelped 9/20/2014. She loves long beach days in the water and especially enjoys cuddling at home. This is Chika's first litter but everyone agrees she is a natural. Her attention to the puppies is unmatched, she still lets them ween even now that their teeth have come in! Like I mentioned, she is just a sweatheart.

Dad
Bruno

Bruno is a Standard Poodle, he was whelped 12/18/2014. Bruno loves kids, he lives with a todler at home and is always careful not to play too rough.
